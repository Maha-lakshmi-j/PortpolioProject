----About us

We Leave A Delicious
Memory For You


Klassy Cafe is one of the best restaurants located in the heart
of Jaipur since 1990. Klassy was started as a small roadside
establishment offering quick servings starting from tea to samosas,
kachoris to masala tikkis and more. We quickly grew into a major
chain of cafes, currently offering over 50 products at 4 outlets
scattered across the city. We are known for taste, quality and quick
service. We also handle orders for home deliveries via Zomato, Swiggy
and Uber Eats. Come, taste the best!
